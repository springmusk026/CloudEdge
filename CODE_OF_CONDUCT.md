# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Standards

**Positive behavior:**
- Being respectful and constructive
- Accepting feedback gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information
- Any conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to springmusk@gmail.com. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated. Maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.
